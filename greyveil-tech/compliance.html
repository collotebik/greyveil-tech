Frameworks We Cover

Built for Africa's Regulatory Reality

📜

Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

Full compliance advisory for Kenya's DPA — data mapping, privacy notices, DPIA support, and ODPC registration. Essential for any Kenyan business handling personal data.

// Required for all Kenyan businesses
🏅

ISO/IEC 27001

The international gold standard for information security management. We guide you from initial gap analysis to successful certification — the mark that opens enterprise and government doors.

// Globally recognised standard
💳

PCI-DSS v4.0

Payment Card Industry compliance for fintechs, banks, and retailers. Essential if you process Visa, Mastercard, or any card transactions. We manage scoping, SAQ, and QSA liaison.

// Required for card processing
🌍

GDPR & Pan-African Frameworks

If you operate beyond Kenya — GDPR for EU data subjects, Nigeria's NDPR, South Africa's POPIA. As you scale across Africa, we scale your compliance with you.
