Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Here are the questions we hear most often. If yours isn't here, just send us a message — we reply to everything.

How quickly can you respond to an active incident?+
For active incidents, call our 24/7 hotline at +254 711 000 000. We have an engineer engaged within 10 minutes and remote containment begins immediately. Do not shut down any systems before speaking to us — this preserves forensic evidence critical to the investigation.
What does the free security assessment include?+
Our free 15-minute assessment covers a high-level review of your attack surface, public-facing assets, and known vulnerability exposure. You'll receive a written summary of your top 3 risk areas within 48 hours — no commitment required.
Do you work with small businesses or only enterprises?+
We work with businesses of all sizes across East Africa. Our pricing is tiered — from focused SME packages starting at KES 85,000 to full enterprise SOC deployments. Every engagement is scoped to your actual needs, not a one-size-fits-all template.
How long does a penetration test take?+
Scope determines duration. A focused web application pentest typically takes 3–5 business days. A full network + infrastructure assessment for a mid-sized organisation runs 7–14 days. We provide a detailed timeline in your scoping proposal before any work begins.
Are you compliant with Kenya's Data Protection Act?+
Yes. All our engagements are conducted within the framework of the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019, and we assist clients in achieving and maintaining DPA compliance. We also support ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, and SOC 2 readiness across our service lines.
Can you work with clients outside Nairobi?+
Absolutely. Our SOC monitoring, cloud security reviews, and most assessment work is delivered remotely — covering clients across Kenya, East Africa, and beyond. For on-site engagements, our team travels to Nairobi and major regional cities as part of the scope.
