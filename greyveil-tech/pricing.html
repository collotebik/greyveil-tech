Pricing Factors

What Shapes Your Quote

01

Scope & Attack Surface

Number of systems, APIs, domains, cloud accounts, and endpoints in scope. More surface area = broader engagement.

02

Industry & Compliance

Regulated sectors (banking, health, government) require additional compliance mapping and documentation.

03

Engagement Duration

One-time audit vs. ongoing SOC retainer vs. quarterly penetration testing — duration and frequency affect cost significantly.

04

Response SLA

Standard 24/7 monitoring vs. guaranteed sub-10-minute incident response. Faster SLAs require deeper team commitment.

05

Remediation Support

Assessment-only vs. full fix-and-verify. Hands-on remediation and re-testing are scoped separately.

06

Organisation Size

Team size, number of locations, and data volume inform the depth of coverage required to be truly effective.
