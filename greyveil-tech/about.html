Why We Exist

Africa is Online.
The Threats Followed.

Africa has added over 500 million internet users in the last decade. Mobile money, cloud banking, e-commerce, and digital government are transforming the continent. But the security infrastructure hasn't kept pace.

Most African businesses rely on outdated tools, underqualified vendors, or no security at all. Global cybersecurity firms don't understand the local threat landscape — the specific malware targeting M-Pesa integrations, the phishing campaigns built in Swahili, the unique attack patterns targeting East African banks.

Greyveil Tech was built to fill that gap. Locally rooted. Globally standard. Built for Africa, by Africa.

The Journey

From Engineer to Security Founder

The path from writing software to protecting it across an entire continent wasn't linear — but it was inevitable.

2018

The Engineering Years

Started as a software engineer, building systems and products for Kenyan startups and SMEs. Wrote clean code. Shipped products. And slowly realised almost none of it was secure.

2020

The Wake-Up Call

A client's system got breached. Customer data exposed. Money lost. The investigation revealed the attack was entirely preventable — basic misconfigurations, default credentials, no monitoring. The vulnerability wasn't technical. It was a knowledge gap.

2022

Going Deep on Security

Pivoted entirely into cybersecurity. Studied offensive and defensive techniques, earned certifications, worked with security teams across East Africa. Learned that the problem wasn't just one client — it was the entire ecosystem.

2024

Building the Solution

Started advising businesses on their security posture. The demand was immediate and overwhelming. It was clear this needed to be a company — not a freelance service, but a proper security firm built for African scale.

2025

Greyveil Tech is Born

Greyveil Tech officially launched in Nairobi, Kenya — with a mission to become the most trusted cybersecurity firm across Africa. One business protected at a time.
